Frequently Asked Questions

Does this require Admin Rights?

No. AutoFlow Plus runs as a portable standard application. It does not require installation or administrator privileges, making it safe for corporate laptops.

Will this keep my VPN active?

Yes. By simulating user input, AutoFlow Plus prevents the system from entering "Idle" state, which stops VPNs and remote sessions (Citrix, RDP) from timing out.

Does it work with Microsoft Teams/Slack?

Yes. The continuous activity input ensures your status remains "Available" or "Active," facilitating better communication availability for your team.

Is it secure?

AutoFlow Plus operates locally. It does not connect to the internet to transmit data, and the input simulation is confined to the standard Windows API.
